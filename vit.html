    • 3 DAYS CELEBRATION OF VIBRANCE

    March 16, 2023 posted by Admin of VIT

    VIBRANCE
    Day-1 of the festival started with a mini marathon along the 10km stretch from Vandalur to Kelambakkam, in a joint effort by the physical education department and health club, and Rotaract club of VIT Chennai, to spread awareness against drug abuse. Seven-hundred men and 600 women participated in the marathon. Sekar Viswanathan, vice-president, VIT University, floated colourful balloons and distributed prizes, certificates and medals to the winners. The inaugural ceremony saw Shivam Dube, prominent member of the Indian cricket team and the IPL team 'Chennai Super Kings', felicitating the winners of the sports events that were held at the University from February 15 to March 2 The winners of the women's marathon were Saranya and R Niraimathy of Muthayammal College, R Abinya Sri-Raja Lakshmi College, M Vinothini - Anna University, and A Sai Sri, B Suganthi, P Srirama Lakshmi, S Swathisree, D Hemanandini and Sakshi Chauhan of VIT University.
    The winners of the men's marathon were Aravindh N and A Dinesh of Muthayammal College of Arts and Science, Pragatheeswaran G, George Fernandes F and S Premnath of Loyola College; Ajith Kumar K and Gandhi of TNP ESU, Baskar P-VIT Vellore, Karnan D, M Jegadeesan, F Aravindh, S Venkitesan and A John Sahaya Soosal of Tambaram Thunderbolts; Vel Kumar - VIT Vellore (alumni), Chandaran A-Thiruvallur University, Girish, Parksunnath K, Ayush and Shasank of VIT Chennai, and Tejurai of IMV. kick. A big part of the festival is the music club, which will offer a platform for both instrumentalists and vocalists through their signature events B Minor and Crescendo respectively. The dance club will get audiences grooving with InSync, a competition based on synchronization, and Switch It Up, a game based on various dance forms. Another big attraction is 'Style-a-thon, an event put together by the designers club and the photography club. In this colorful contest participants will style costumes based on a theme. As a part of Vibrance'23's pro-shows, Bollywood singers Shirley Setia and Benny Dayal gave power-packed performances on Day 1. With numerous club events, stage performances, food stalls and exhibits, Vibrance 23 promises to be a melting pot of young talent and energy.
